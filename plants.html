A Guiltless Pleasure

How I became a plant person

I'm going to start at the very beginning... just kidding i've only been in this hobby for about 2 years, but how did I get into it you may ask?

It all started with a roomate, who I thought at the time had way too many plants... Maybe, I wouldn't have had an issue if there wasn't dirt in the randomest places, and if only she would have cared enough to brush the mats out of her cats fur. And so, to be honest, I wasn't seeing plants in a good light. But it left me wondering, why did she like plants so much?

After realizing that I was a newly-wed in a duplex that didn't allow pets, not even a fish; my need to take care things told me that I needed to keep looking. Then my husband suggested the brilliant idea that I get a plant. I wasn't too sure at first but then the idea started to grow on me. I started to watch a lot of plant videos on youtube, and started becoming obsessed with plants. At the plant store, I fell in love with a small peace-lily with beautiful dark leaves and the potential to give me beautiful white blooms. With my husband by my side, he decided to gift it to me, and thats how one plant turned into ten more.
